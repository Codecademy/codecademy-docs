The **`fa-spin`** class causes any icon to rotate.

## Example

```html
<i class="fas fa-circle-notch fa-spin"></i>
<i class="fas fa-sync-alt fa-spin"></i>
<i class="fas fa-cog fa-spin"></i>
```
