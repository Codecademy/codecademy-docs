The `exp()` function Returns E raised to the power of x (E^x^).

## Syntax

```pseudo

exp($x)

```

- `$x`: specifies the exponent that E will be raised to.

## Codebyte Example

```codebyte/php
<?php
  echo (exp(0)."\n");
  echo (exp(1). "\n");
  echo (exp(2). "\n");
  echo (exp(4.8). "\n");
  echo (exp(10). "\n");
?>
```
