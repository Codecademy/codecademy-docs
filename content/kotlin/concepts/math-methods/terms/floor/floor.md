**Title:** `floor()`
**Description:** " The `floor()` method returns the largest integer that is less than or equal to the argument. "

**Syntax:** 
```kotlin
kotlin.math.floor(x: Double)
```

**Example:** 
```kotlin
val x = 3.14
val y = 3.99
println(floor(x)) // 3.0
println(floor(y)) // 3.0
```
